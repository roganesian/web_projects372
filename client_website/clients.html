Client Details

BJs

BJ's Wholesale Club is a leading warehouse club chain offering a wide range of products at competitive prices. Partnering with BJ's allows Enerjy to reach a broader audience of health-conscious consumers who value quality and affordability. By offering our fresh and healthy Mediterranean alternatives in BJ's stores, we can provide convenient access to nutritious meals for families and individuals seeking wholesome food options.

Costco Wholesale

Costco Wholesale is renowned for its high-quality products and commitment to customer satisfaction. Collaborating with Costco aligns with Enerjy's mission to deliver fresh and healthy Mediterranean cuisine to a diverse customer base. By showcasing our offerings in Costco's stores, we can introduce our delicious and nutritious menu items to Costco members, providing them with convenient access to flavorful and wholesome dining options.

Daves Marketplace

Dave's Marketplace is a local grocery chain known for its dedication to supporting local businesses and offering fresh, locally sourced products. Partnering with Dave's Marketplace allows Enerjy to connect with the community and promote our commitment to providing fresh and healthy Mediterranean dishes. By collaborating with Dave's Marketplace, we can offer our customers a taste of authentic Mediterranean cuisine while supporting local growers and producers.

Stop and Shop

Stop & Shop is a trusted supermarket chain committed to providing customers with a wide selection of high-quality products and exceptional service. Teaming up with Stop & Shop enables Enerjy to expand its reach and offer our nutritious and delicious Mediterranean options to a larger audience. By partnering with Stop & Shop, we can introduce our fresh and healthy menu items to shoppers looking for convenient and satisfying meal solutions, furthering our mission to promote wholesome eating and well-being.
